Delete Your Alembic Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our system.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your login credentials, email address, and name
  • Course Data: Any courses you've uploaded or created (if applicable)
  • Preferences and Analytics: Your learning preferences, usage history, and personalized recommendations
  • User-Generated Content: Any notes, comments, or feedback you've provided

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account in two ways:

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open the Alembic app
  2. Navigate to Settings â†’ Account Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Method 2: Contact Support

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  • Email: xtc021@qq.com

Processing Time

Your account deletion request will be processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, security).

Important Notes

  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your courses and learning progress
  • Any active subscriptions or purchases will be cancelled
  • After deletion, you may create a new account, but previous data cannot be recovered
